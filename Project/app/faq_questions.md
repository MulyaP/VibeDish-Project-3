# VibeDish FAQ - Common User Questions

## Order Tracking (15 questions)

1. **How can I track my order in real-time?**
   Go to "My Orders" in the app and select your active order. You'll see real-time status updates including "confirmed", "preparing", "ready", "picked up", and "delivered".

2. **Why is my order status still showing "preparing" after 30 minutes?**
   Restaurants update status manually. During peak hours or for complex orders, preparation may take longer. Contact the restaurant directly if it exceeds estimated time.

3. **Can I see the delivery driver's location on the map?**
   Yes, once your order status changes to "picked up", you can view the driver's real-time location on the integrated Mapbox map.

4. **What does "ready for pickup" status mean?**
   Your order is prepared and waiting at the restaurant for a delivery driver to collect it. A driver will be assigned shortly.

5. **How long does delivery usually take?**
   Typical delivery time is 30-45 minutes, including preparation and delivery. This varies based on restaurant location, order complexity, and driver availability.

6. **Why hasn't my order moved from "confirmed" status?**
   The restaurant may be busy or experiencing delays. If it's been over 15 minutes, contact the restaurant or our support team.

7. **Can I get notifications when my order status changes?**
   Yes, enable push notifications in your app settings to receive real-time updates for each status change.

8. **What happens if my delivery is delayed?**
   You'll receive automatic notifications about delays. Significant delays may qualify for delivery fee refunds or credits.

9. **How do I know when my driver has picked up my order?**
   Your order status will change to "picked up" and you'll receive a notification with the driver's details and estimated arrival time.

10. **Can I contact my delivery driver directly?**
    Yes, once assigned, you can call or message your driver through the app's built-in communication system.

11. **Why is the estimated delivery time different from what I was told initially?**
    Initial estimates are based on average times. Real-time factors like traffic, weather, and restaurant preparation speed can affect actual delivery time.

12. **What does "out for delivery" mean exactly?**
    Your driver has picked up your order from the restaurant and is en route to your delivery address.

13. **How accurate is the GPS tracking for my order?**
    Our Mapbox integration provides accurate real-time tracking with updates every 30 seconds when your order is out for delivery.

14. **Can I change my delivery address after the order is confirmed?**
    Address changes are only possible before the restaurant starts preparing your order. Contact support immediately for assistance.

15. **What should I do if my order shows delivered but I haven't received it?**
    Check with neighbors or building reception. If still missing, report it immediately through the app for investigation and refund processing.

## Payment Issues (12 questions)

16. **Why was my payment declined?**
    Common reasons include insufficient funds, expired card, incorrect billing address, or bank security blocks. Try a different payment method or contact your bank.

17. **Can I use multiple payment methods for one order?**
    Currently, only one payment method per order is supported. You can use gift cards or credits in combination with a primary payment method.

18. **How do I add a new credit card to my account?**
    Go to Account Settings > Payment Methods > Add New Card. Enter your card details securely through our encrypted payment system.

19. **Why was I charged twice for the same order?**
    This may be a temporary authorization hold. If both charges remain after 3-5 business days, contact support for immediate resolution.

20. **Can I pay with cash on delivery?**
    Cash payments are not currently supported. We accept credit/debit cards and digital payment methods for security and efficiency.

21. **How do I update my default payment method?**
    Go to Account Settings > Payment Methods, select your preferred card, and tap "Set as Default".

22. **What payment methods do you accept?**
    We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, and digital wallets. All payments are processed securely through Supabase.

23. **Why is there a pending charge on my card?**
    We place a temporary authorization hold when you place an order. This becomes a final charge once your order is confirmed.

24. **Can I use gift cards or promo codes?**
    Yes, enter promo codes at checkout. Gift card functionality is available in Account Settings > Credits & Gift Cards.

25. **How do I remove a saved payment method?**
    Go to Account Settings > Payment Methods, select the card you want to remove, and tap "Delete". You must have at least one payment method saved.

26. **Why am I seeing additional fees I wasn't told about?**
    All fees (delivery, service, taxes) are displayed before order confirmation. Check your order summary for a complete breakdown.

27. **Can I split the bill with friends?**
    Bill splitting is not currently available. One person must pay for the entire order, but you can request reimbursement from friends separately.

## Order Cancellation (10 questions)

28. **How do I cancel my order?**
    Go to "My Orders", select your active order, and tap "Cancel Order". Cancellation availability depends on order status.

29. **Can I cancel after the restaurant has started preparing my food?**
    Cancellation becomes difficult once preparation begins. You may be charged a cancellation fee or receive partial refund depending on preparation progress.

30. **What's the cancellation policy?**
    Free cancellation within 5 minutes of ordering. After that, cancellation fees may apply based on order status and restaurant policy.

31. **How long do I have to cancel my order?**
    You have 5 minutes for free cancellation. After this window, cancellation depends on restaurant acceptance and may incur fees.

32. **Can I cancel if the delivery is taking too long?**
    Yes, excessive delays (over 60 minutes past estimated time) qualify for free cancellation with full refund.

33. **What happens if the restaurant cancels my order?**
    You'll receive immediate notification and automatic full refund. We'll also provide credits for the inconvenience.

34. **Can I modify my order instead of canceling it?**
    Order modifications are only possible within 5 minutes of placing the order and before restaurant confirmation.

35. **Why can't I cancel my order through the app?**
    If cancellation option is unavailable, your order may already be in preparation. Contact support for assistance.

36. **What if I need to cancel due to an emergency?**
    Contact our support team immediately. Emergency cancellations are handled case-by-case with maximum flexibility.

37. **Can I cancel just one item from my order?**
    Partial cancellations are only possible within the first 5 minutes and if the restaurant hasn't started preparation.

## Refunds (12 questions)

38. **How do I request a refund for my order?**
    Go to Order History, select the problematic order, tap "Report Issue", and choose the appropriate reason. Provide details and photos if applicable.

39. **How long does it take to process a refund?**
    Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. You'll receive confirmation once the refund is initiated.

40. **Can I get a partial refund for missing items?**
    Yes, you'll receive a refund for the exact cost of missing items plus applicable taxes.

41. **What's your refund policy for late deliveries?**
    Deliveries over 60 minutes past estimated time qualify for delivery fee refunds. Extreme delays may warrant full order refunds.

42. **Why was my refund request denied?**
    Refunds may be denied for subjective taste preferences, orders delivered within estimated time, or insufficient evidence of issues.

43. **Can I get a refund if I don't like the food?**
    Taste preference refunds are generally not provided unless there's a clear quality issue (cold, spoiled, or incorrectly prepared food).

44. **How do I get refunded for wrong items?**
    Report wrong items immediately through the app with photos. You'll receive a full refund for incorrect items.

45. **Will I get a full refund if my order is cancelled?**
    Yes, cancelled orders receive full refunds including all fees, regardless of who initiated the cancellation.

46. **Can I get store credit instead of a refund?**
    Yes, you can choose between refund to original payment method or VibeDish credits (often with bonus value).

47. **What if my food arrived cold or spoiled?**
    Report immediately with photos. Cold or spoiled food qualifies for full refund plus potential credits for the inconvenience.

48. **How do I dispute a charge on my card?**
    First contact our support team. If unresolved, you can dispute with your bank, but we'll work to resolve issues directly first.

49. **Can I get a refund for delivery fees?**
    Delivery fee refunds are provided for late deliveries, cancelled orders, or significant service issues.

## Missing Items (8 questions)

50. **What should I do if items are missing from my order?**
    Check your order immediately upon delivery. Report missing items through the app within 30 minutes for fastest resolution.

51. **How do I report missing items?**
    Go to Order History > Select Order > "Report Issue" > "Missing Items". Select the missing items and submit your report.

52. **Can I get a refund for missing items?**
    Yes, you'll receive an immediate refund for the cost of missing items plus applicable taxes and fees.

53. **Why do items sometimes go missing?**
    Common causes include restaurant packing errors, high-volume periods, complex orders, or miscommunication between kitchen and packaging staff.

54. **Can you redeliver just the missing items?**
    Redelivery depends on restaurant availability and distance. We typically provide refunds or credits as it's faster than redelivery.

55. **How do I prove that items were missing?**
    Take photos of your delivered order and receipt. Our system tracks ordered vs. delivered items for verification.

56. **What if the restaurant forgot to include sauces or utensils?**
    Report missing condiments, utensils, or napkins through the app. While small items, we'll provide credits for the inconvenience.

57. **Can I get credit for future orders instead of a refund?**
    Yes, you can choose VibeDish credits instead of refunds. Credits often include bonus value and never expire.

## Account Issues (15 questions)

58. **How do I reset my password?**
    On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password", enter your email, and follow the reset link sent to your email.

59. **Why can't I log into my account?**
    Check your email/password, ensure caps lock is off, clear app cache, or try password reset. Contact support if issues persist.

60. **How do I change my email address?**
    Go to Account Settings > Profile > Email Address. You'll need to verify the new email address before the change takes effect.

61. **Can I delete my account permanently?**
    Yes, go to Account Settings > Privacy > Delete Account. This action is irreversible and removes all data permanently.

62. **Why is my account suspended?**
    Suspensions occur due to policy violations, fraudulent activity, or excessive refund requests. Contact support for specific details.

63. **How do I update my profile information?**
    Go to Account Settings > Profile to update name, phone number, delivery addresses, and preferences.

64. **Can I have multiple accounts with the same email?**
    No, each email address can only be associated with one VibeDish account for security and order tracking purposes.

65. **How do I change my phone number?**
    Go to Account Settings > Profile > Phone Number. You'll receive a verification code at your new number to confirm the change.

66. **Why am I not receiving verification codes?**
    Check spam folder, ensure correct phone number, verify network connection, or try requesting a new code after 60 seconds.

67. **How do I enable two-factor authentication?**
    Go to Account Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication and follow the setup instructions using your preferred method.

68. **Can I merge two accounts?**
    Account merging is not automatically available. Contact support with both account details for manual assistance.

69. **How do I update my delivery preferences?**
    Go to Account Settings > Delivery Preferences to set default addresses, delivery instructions, and contact preferences.

70. **Why can't I access my order history?**
    Try refreshing the app, checking your internet connection, or logging out and back in. Contact support if the issue persists.

71. **How do I change my notification settings?**
    Go to Account Settings > Notifications to customize push notifications, email alerts, and SMS preferences.

72. **What should I do if someone else is using my account?**
    Immediately change your password, enable two-factor authentication, check recent orders, and contact support to secure your account.

## Restaurant Issues (10 questions)

73. **What should I do if a restaurant is closed but still accepting orders?**
    Report this immediately through the app. We'll contact the restaurant and provide a full refund if they cannot fulfill your order.

74. **Why is my favorite restaurant not available?**
    Restaurants may be temporarily closed, outside delivery radius, experiencing high volume, or updating their menu/hours.

75. **How do I report a problem with a restaurant?**
    Go to the restaurant's page and tap "Report Issue" or contact support with specific details about the problem.

76. **Can I leave a review for the restaurant?**
    Yes, after order completion, you can rate and review restaurants in your Order History. Reviews help other users make informed choices.

77. **Why are some menu items unavailable?**
    Items may be out of stock, seasonal, or temporarily unavailable due to supply issues. Restaurants update availability in real-time.

78. **What if the restaurant made my order wrong?**
    Report incorrect orders immediately through the app with photos. You'll receive refunds for wrong items and may get redelivery.

79. **How do I contact the restaurant directly?**
    Restaurant contact information is available on their profile page, but most issues are better handled through our support system.

80. **Why are delivery times longer for certain restaurants?**
    Factors include distance, preparation complexity, restaurant capacity, and current order volume. Popular restaurants may have longer wait times.

81. **Can I request special instructions for my order?**
    Yes, add special instructions in the "Notes" section during checkout. However, complex modifications may not always be possible.

82. **What if the restaurant's food quality is poor?**
    Report quality issues with photos and details. Poor quality (cold, stale, or improperly prepared food) qualifies for refunds.

## Spotify Integration (18 questions)

83. **How do I connect my Spotify account to VibeDish?**
    Go to Account Settings > Connected Apps > Spotify and tap "Connect". You'll be redirected to Spotify to authorize access.

84. **Why can't VibeDish access my Spotify data?**
    Check if you've authorized access, ensure your Spotify account is active, and verify your internet connection. Try reconnecting if needed.

85. **What happens if I don't have Spotify Premium?**
    Spotify Free works fine! We only need access to your listening history and currently playing track, which are available on free accounts.

86. **How does mood-based food recommendation work?**
    Our AI analyzes your current/recent music using Groq API to determine mood, then matches it with surplus meals that complement your vibe.

87. **Can I disconnect Spotify from my VibeDish account?**
    Yes, go to Account Settings > Connected Apps > Spotify > Disconnect. You can still use VibeDish without mood-based recommendations.

88. **Why are the food recommendations not matching my mood?**
    Music taste is subjective. Try refreshing recommendations, ensure you're actively listening, or manually browse surplus meals instead.

89. **What if I'm not currently listening to music?**
    We'll analyze your recent listening history (last 24 hours) to determine your general mood and preferences.

90. **How does VibeDish analyze my music taste?**
    We use Groq AI to analyze song attributes like tempo, energy, valence, and genre to determine emotional state and food preferences.

91. **Can I manually set my mood instead of using Spotify?**
    Currently, mood detection is Spotify-based only. Manual mood selection is planned for future updates.

92. **Why do I need to authorize Spotify access?**
    Authorization ensures secure, permission-based access to your listening data while protecting your privacy and account security.

93. **What Spotify data does VibeDish use?**
    We access currently playing track, recent listening history, and basic profile info. We never access playlists, saved music, or personal data.

94. **Can I use Apple Music or other streaming services?**
    Currently only Spotify is supported. Apple Music and other platforms may be added based on user demand.

95. **How often does VibeDish check my Spotify activity?**
    We check when you open the app or refresh recommendations. We don't continuously monitor your listening activity.

96. **What if my Spotify account is private?**
    Private accounts work fine! We only access data you've explicitly authorized, not your public profile or social features.

97. **Can I see how my music translates to food recommendations?**
    Yes, tap the "Why this recommendation?" link on suggested meals to see the mood analysis and reasoning.

98. **Why are my recommendations always the same?**
    Try listening to different music genres, refresh the recommendations, or check if surplus meal availability is limited in your area.

99. **How do I refresh my mood-based recommendations?**
    Pull down on the recommendations screen or tap the refresh icon to get updated suggestions based on current music.

100. **What if I share my Spotify account with family members?**
     Shared accounts may affect recommendation accuracy. Consider creating individual Spotify accounts or using VibeDish without mood-based features.
